Rahul Rai

The man who took us to another realm of music

Time line of Rahul rai

  • 1989 - born in Kolkota, India
  • 1992 – developed his taste in music and created a heavy metal band named “dream diabolic ”
  • 2009 – won on the regional level interschool event “Rock-o-phoenic” and also won the title of “Regional Campus Rock Idol”.

    • (dream diabolic disbanded during this period and he returned back to his hometown)

  • 2013 - tribal rain was formed by him and his friends
  • 2016 - Chinta was released his debut single. It is a song about the worries and anxieties of life and how to overcome them with positivity and hope
  • 2017 - Bhanai was his second single, a song about the importance of communication and understanding in relationships
  • 2018 -Narisauna was his third single, a song about the pain of losing someone you love and the struggle to move on
  • 2018 - passed away in a tragic incident. He was found dead in his home in Namchi, Sikkim, in an apparent suicide
  • 2022 - Sahara was his last single, released posthumously. A song about the need for support and comfort in times of hardship and loneliness
